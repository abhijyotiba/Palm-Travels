About Palm Travels

Your trusted partner in creating unforgettable travel experiences since 2010

Our Story

Born from a passion for exploration and discovery

Palm Travels began as a dream shared by a group of passionate travelers who believed that every journey should be extraordinary. Founded in 2010, we started with a simple mission: to make the world more accessible and help people create memories that last a lifetime.

What started as a small local travel agency has grown into a trusted partner for thousands of travelers worldwide. Our founders, Sarah and Michael Johnson, combined their extensive travel experience with a commitment to personalized service, creating a company that truly understands what makes a trip special.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 5,000 travelers explore more than 150 destinations across the globe. Our team of dedicated travel experts continues to grow, but our core values remain the same: authenticity, quality, and genuine care for our travelers' experiences.

Our Mission

To inspire and enable people to explore the world through exceptional travel experiences that are thoughtfully designed, responsibly delivered, and personally meaningful.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted travel partner, connecting people with authentic experiences while promoting sustainable and responsible tourism practices.

Our Values

Authenticity, sustainability, personalized service, and creating meaningful connections between travelers and the destinations they visit.
